Computer Vision

In the application of Generative AI, its practical use in the field of computer vision has a very important role. It not only generates new images and videos, but also has the ability to synthesise images, enhance image resolution and so on. These techniques have saved resources and injected new energy into many areas of digital media.

Natural Language Processing

Another landmark advancement of Generative AI in digital media is the creation of Natural Language Processing, and these AI-generated content have gained widespread attention, with ChatGPT being the most well-known. Unlike content created by human authors, AI-generated content is characterised by the creation of large amounts of relevant content in a short period of time.

Music Production

With the advent of generative AI, the way music is created and consumed is changing. This technology allows machines to produce entirely new music without any human intervention. MusicLM, which is developed by Google, can generate music based on textual descriptions and in different formats. Suno AI can quickly generate entire songs from simple text prompts, including vocals, instruments, lyrics, and even titles, across a range of genres.

Game Development

One of the most remarkable accomplishments has been the implementation of generative AI to provide more adaptable interactivity for non-playable characters (NPCs) within games. By using ChatGPT or other models, game NPCs are no longer traditional characters that can only reply in fixed and stiff-toned dialogue, but can instead generate emotional responses.

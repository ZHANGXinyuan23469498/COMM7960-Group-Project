About Generative AI

Generative AI refers to a category of artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms that generate new outputs based on the data they have been trained on. Unlike traditional AI systems that are designed to recognize patterns and make predictions, generative AI creates new content in the form of images, text, audio, and more.


Some popular Generative AI models


Transformer

Transformer has been successful in natural language processing as the backbone structure of many state-of-the-art models such as GPT-3, DALL-E-2, Codex and others. It is based on self-attention mechanism and mainly consists of an encoder which receives the input sequence and produces the hidden representation and a decoder which receives the hidden representation and produces the output sequence.The Transformer model can generate coherent text.

GANs

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) are widely used in the field of image generation.GAN is composed of two parts, generator which learns from the distribution of real examples and generates synthetic samples, and discriminator which is responsible for distinguishing between real and fake samples. The competing steps of these two parts can help in generating realistic data.

VAEs

Variational Autoencoders (VAE) is a generative model where the encoder maps the input data to the latent space and the decoder is responsible for reconstructing the data from the latent space. It reflects the data into a probability distribution and studies the reconstruction which is close to its raw input.
